BACKGROUND INFORMATION

Early Interest

My journey into the world of animation and game development began with my love for anime. Watching series like "One Piece" and movies from Studio Ghibli captivated my imagination and inspired me to pursue a career where I could create such captivating and heartfelt stories.


Educational Path

Currently, I am studying for a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology, majoring in Animation and Game Development at Far Eastern University - Institute of Technology. This program has provided me with a solid foundation in both technical skills and creative processes, allowing me to explore various aspects of animation and game development.


GOALS

Career Aspirations

I aspire to work for renowned animation studios like One Piece and Studio Ghibli. My ultimate goal is to be part of a team that creates stories and animations that leave a lasting impact on audiences. I want to contribute to projects that blend compelling narratives with stunning visuals, evoking emotions and inspiring others just as I have been inspired.


Specific Interests

In the realm of animation, I am particularly fascinated by character design and storytelling. I aim to develop my skills in these areas to create characters that resonate with viewers and stories that captivate and engage. In game development, I am interested in creating immersive experiences that blend gameplay with narrative elements, offering players meaningful and memorable interactions.
